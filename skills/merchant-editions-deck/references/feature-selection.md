# Feature Selection Guide

Use this reference to choose merchant-relevant Shopify Editions Spring ’26 features.

## Merchant fit patterns

### Large catalogue / variant-heavy merchants

Prioritise:
- Shopify Catalog / richer product data
- Improved storefront search
- Variant-level product publishing
- Online Store Agent / AI assistant
- Rollouts for theme/checkout testing
- Native inventory management
- Inventory API concurrency protection

Why:
Large catalogues depend on search, product data quality, availability rules, and merchandising experiments.

### Premium DTC / lifestyle brands

Prioritise:
- Agentic selling hub
- Shopify Catalog / richer product data
- Personalized shopping assistant in Shop
- Improved storefront search
- Tighter, cleaner checkout
- Refreshed customer accounts
- Marketing consent on sign-in
- Rollouts for theme/checkout testing
- Local currency gift cards

Why:
Premium shoppers need confidence, inspiration, branded journeys, smooth checkout, and retention moments.

### International / multi-market merchants

Prioritise:
- Discounts by market
- Local/regional payment methods in Shop Pay
- Local currency gift cards
- Multi-entity support
- Better address suggestions
- Variant-level publishing
- Shopify Catalog

Why:
Global growth creates complexity in currency, payments, promotions, availability, delivery, and product data.

### Retail / omnichannel merchants

Prioritise:
- Fastest-ever Shopify POS
- Faster POS search
- Returns and exchanges in one cart
- Offline checkout / resilient checkout
- POS UI extensions offline
- Shop + retail bridge
- Split fulfilment
- Native inventory management

Why:
Retail merchants care about checkout speed, store continuity, returns, inventory and connecting online/store journeys.

### B2B / trade merchants

Prioritise:
- Shopify Catalog / richer product data
- Universal Commerce Protocol / checkout in AI conversations
- Flexible / stackable discounts
- Metaobject access in Checkout Functions
- VAT ID validation at checkout
- Customer accounts
- Variant-level publishing
- Native inventory management

Why:
B2B merchants need pricing, eligibility, VAT, compliance, product restrictions, and repeat-purchase workflows.

### Promo-heavy / seasonal merchants

Prioritise:
- Combine multiple discounts on a product
- Discounts by market
- Discount × campaign attribution
- Rollouts for testing
- Marketing consent on sign-in
- Analytics annotations
- Metric targets

Why:
Promo-heavy merchants need clean promo setup, margin control and measurement.

## Plain-English notes rules

For every feature, notes must answer:

- What does it do?
- What is the dummies version?
- Why does this matter to this specific merchant?
- What can Hannah say live?
- What question should Hannah ask?

Avoid vague notes like “improves experience”. Tie to merchant realities: variants, markets, stores, gifting, returns, repeat purchase, product data, search, checkout, margins, inventory, or support load.
